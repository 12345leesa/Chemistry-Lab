S Block


Location in the Periodic Table

  • Group 1: Alkali metals (Li, Na, K, Rb, Cs,Fr.)
  • Group 2: Alkaline earth metals (Be, Mg, Ca, Sr,Ba, Ra.)
  • Their outermost electrons are in the s-orbital.

S block Elements are ->

    Lithium (Li), Sodium (Na), Potassium (K), Rubidium (Rb), Cesium (Cs), Francium (Fr), Beryllium (Be), Magnesium (Mg), Calcium (Ca), Strontium (Sr), Barium (Ba), Radium (Ra),

Key Characteristics

  • Group 1: Ends with ns¹ (e.g., Lithium: 1s² 2s¹).
  • Group 2: Ends with ns² (e.g., Magnesium: 1s² 2s² 2p⁶ 3s²).
  • Reactivity: Increases down the group.
  • Compounds: Mostly ionic with +1 or +2 oxidation states.

Trends in the Groups

  • Atomic Radius: Increases down the group.
  • Ionization Energy: Decreases down the group.
  • Melting/Boiling Points: Decrease down the group for Group 1.
