Step-by-Step Flame Test Guide

Step 1

Clean a wire loop by dipping it in hydrochloric acid and heating it until no color is produced in the flame.

↓
Step 2

Dip the wire loop into the sample of the compound to be tested.

↓
Step 3

Hold the wire loop over the Bunsen burner flame and observe the color produced.

↓
Step 4

Compare the color with the known flame colors to identify the metal ion.
