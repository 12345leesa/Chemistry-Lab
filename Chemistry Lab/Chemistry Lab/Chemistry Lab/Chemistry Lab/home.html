Periodic Table

The periodic table, developed by Dmitri Mendeleev in 1869, organizes elements by atomic number and properties.Elements are grouped into metals, nonmetals, and metalloids, and trends such as electronegativity, ionization energy, and atomic radius can be observed.It plays a crucial role in predicting chemical behavior and understanding atomic structure.
